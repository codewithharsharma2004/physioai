What is AI Physio?

AI Physio is an innovative web-based application that uses advanced artificial intelligence and computer vision to provide real-time guidance for physiotherapy exercises. Using TensorFlow.js and MoveNet pose detection, our AI analyzes your movements and provides instant, personalized feedback to help you perform exercises correctly.
